Frequently asked questions
What will happen if my parcel will get lost?

What will happen if my parcel will get lost? Insurance company covers full or partial amount of the cost for the lost parcel (it can be a box, luggage bag etc).

How do I package my parcel?

If you have chosen to package your parcel yourself then you have to make sure that you will apply following criteria:

1. Your parcel must be sealed properly with tape, cling film or boxed and taped. It must be stable and should not get ripped while transportation.

2. If you parcel is fragile item and you have chosen to pack it your self then it must be covered with bubble wrap and placed in to the crafted or wooden box. Fragile tape mast be applied, UPD logistics does not take responsibility of broken item.

3. Each of your parcel must be covered and labelled with your unique number which shown on your declaration form.

Do you offer safe packaging for the fragile goods?

Yes we do offer safe packaging which keeps your fragile item safe and secure. Extra cost will be applied for special packaging of your fragile goods. you can find the UDP Logistics fees by clicking here

What is restricted to send?
Please note that all shipments of personal goods MUST NOT contain any of the following items without being declared to UDP Logistics and pre-arrangements being made to transport them safely and in accordance with IATA/ICAO dangerous goods regulations.

Lithium Ion Batteries and items containing lithium ion batteries
(e.g. Mobile Phones/Laptop Computers/Cordless power tools)

Petrol or diesel powered equipment with residue of fuel
(e.g. generators, chainsaws etc.) -Aerosols ( e.g. deodorant sprays)

Flammable liquids
(e.g. perfumes/paint)

Flammable solids
(e.g. matches)

Flammable gases
(e.g. lighter fuel)

(e.g. acid/car batteries)

(e.g. fireworks)
